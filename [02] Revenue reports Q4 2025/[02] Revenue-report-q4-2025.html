Executive Summary

Q4 2025 closed with total revenue of $58.9M, a 20.9% increase year-over-year and 7.8% above internal targets. Gross margin expanded to 71.6%, fueled by platform automation gains and the full ramp of the Enterprise Plus tier. EBITDA reached $21.2M with a margin of 36.0%, up from 32.4% in Q4 2024. Net income hit $13.1M, marking the tenth consecutive quarter of profitability growth. Operating expenses held at 33.8% of revenue, a 2.2pp improvement year-over-year, reflecting disciplined cost management across all departments while accelerating top-line growth.
