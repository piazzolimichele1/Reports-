Confidential

Comparative: Q2 2022 → Q2 2025

Executive Summary

This report presents a four-year comparative analysis of Q2 operational performance at Skygate International Airport. Q2 2025 represents the strongest quarter on record, with total passengers reaching 14.82M — a 56.4% increase from Q2 2022's 9.48M. Aircraft movements grew from 72,400 to 98,640 over the same period, a 36.2% expansion. On-time performance improved steadily from 81.2% to 87.4%, reflecting cumulative investments in ground operations, ATC coordination, and predictive scheduling. Cargo volume rose from 142,600t to 186,400t, with 2025 marking the first quarter above 180,000t. Revenue per passenger increased from $14.20 to $18.40, driven by terminal modernization and expanded commercial offerings.
